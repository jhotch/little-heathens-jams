Hand-Made and Local

Established in 2010, Little Heathen's is an artisan jam propriety located in Des Moines, Iowa. I use small businesses and local farms to make my products. These recipes showcase the culinary ambition of a Foodie! Jams are not just for toast, you can make something good into something great with Little Heathen's!

Natural

Natural ingredients and organic cane sugar are used in all my recipes. No corn syrup and no GMOs! Gourmet herbs and spices are used from our local culinarium or my own garden. The pectin used for gelling is 100% organic citrus pectin! Using organic citrus pectin means half the sugar and a beautiful product every time!

Bold, New Recipes and Old Favorites

Ever tried strawberry Riesling jam? How about blueberry preserves with pure maple syrup or a spicy jam made with Bing cherries and habanero? I have the old favorites too; strawberry rhubarb, apple butter and Concord grape jam; just to name a few. Browse my selections in my online store!
