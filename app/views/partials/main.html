Hand-Made and Local

Established in 2010, Little Heathen's is an artisan jam propriety located in Des Moines, Iowa. I use small businesses and local farms to make my products and grow many of the herbs used in the recipes I have created; otherwise I procure gourmet ingredients from my local culinarium. I want consumers to have a _______ when they try my jam.

Natural

I use natural ingredients and organic cane sugar in my recipes. No corn syrup, no GMOs, even the pectin I use to make the jams gel is 100% organic citrus pectin! Using organic citrus pectin means half the sugar and a beautiful product every time!

Bold, New Recipes and Old Favorites

Ever tried strawberry Riesling jam? How about blueberry preserves with pure maple syrup or a spicy jam made with Bing cherries and habanero? I have the old favorites too; strawberry rhubarb, apple butter and Concord grape jam; just to name a few. Browse my selections in my online store!
